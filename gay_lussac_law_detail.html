   Gay-Lussac Law exercises   

  1. Determine the pressure change when 3 moles of nitrogen gas at a constant volume at 1.00 atm is heated from 20.0 °C to 30.0 °C.
  2. A gas has a pressure of 0.370 atm at 50.0 °C. What is the pressure at standard temperature?
  3. A gas has a pressure of 699.0 mm Hg at 40.0 °C. What is the temperature at standard pressure?
  4. A 30.0 L sample of nitrogen inside a rigid, metal container at 20.0 °C is placed inside an oven whose temperature is 50.0 °C. The pressure inside the container at 20.0 °C was at 3.00 atm. What is the pressure of the nitrogen after its temperature is increased?
  5. A sample of gas at 3.00 x 103 mm Hg inside a steel tank is cooled from 500.0 °C to 0.00 °C. What is the final pressure of the gas in the steel tank?
  6. The temperature of a sample of gas in a steel container at 30.0 kPa is increased from -100.0 °C to 1.00 x 103 °C. What is the final pressure inside the tank?
  7. Calculate the final pressure inside a scuba tank after it cools from 1.00 x 103 °C to 25.0 °C. The initial pressure in the tank is 130.0 atm.
