   Game of Chaos    


Use the Python programming language to simulate the Game of Chaos using the following rules:

  1. Choose a random starting point within the triangle ABC
  2. Roll die
  3. Connect the point
    • with edge A, when you roll a 1 or 2
    • with edge B, when you roll a 3 or 4
    • with edge C, when you roll a 5 or 6
  4. Repeat step 2
